Norminal
An app for everything about SpaceX.
Follow launches, astronauts and payloads, directly from your iPhone.

I'm creating this app in order to have every possible information about SpaceX launches organized in a tidy and native-style user interface. Everything is organized in cards like the ones you see in the App Store. Since I care about details, each astronaut picture gets cropped with a face detection system in order to have every one of them aligned inside a circle, inspired from the contacts list. Every picture is double cached for optimization, in order to have quicker loading times and better drawing performance.

This is my first public iOS app, and it's made with SwiftUI.
